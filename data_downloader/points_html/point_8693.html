Terms of Use
Aside from sexually explicit, violent, discriminatory, unlawful, illegal, or spam conduct, SparkNotes may terminate your access to the service if your conduct/communications are "deemed by us to be unsuitable for any other reason". They may deem it to be unsuitable even if it is based on opinion, suspicion, or belief, even if it is not well-founded or if other users are allowed access despite having conduct/communications similar to yours.
